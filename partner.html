The Opportunity: Transforming Indian Agriculture

Indian agriculture is at a tipping point. With 150 million farmers contributing to 15% of GDP, the sector is ripe for technological disruption that can create immense value while solving critical social challenges.

Massive Market Gap

  • Limited digital access for 85% of smallholder farmers
  • Inefficient supply chains causing 30-40% post-harvest losses
  • Limited access to formal credit and insurance
  • Fragmented market with limited price transparency

Our Solution

  • Jama App - Digital platform for farmers in local languages
  • Market linkage connecting farmers directly to buyers
  • Access to credit, insurance, and government schemes
  • Data-driven insights for better crop planning

Sustainable Business Model

Our multi-pronged revenue approach ensures sustainable growth while creating maximum impact for farmers.

Transaction Fees

Small commission on marketplace transactions between farmers and buyers.

Projected: 40% of revenue

Premium Subscriptions

Advanced features for serious farmers including detailed analytics and premium support.

Projected: 25% of revenue

Enterprise Services

Data analytics and insights for agribusinesses, insurers, and financial institutions.

Projected: 35% of revenue
